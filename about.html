About Me

I was born in northeast Ohio and raised with a deep passion for mathematics and sports. I studied at the Illinois Institute of Technology and later at Cornell University, where I embarked on a journey that led me to research and innovation. My career has evolved from academia to the tech industry, with notable work at institutions such as Argonne National Labs and SigOpt.

Growing Up & Moving Around

My journey spans from my hometown in Ohio, to Chicago, Cornell, Argonne, and beyond. I cherish the time spent in Hong Kong and the personal connections forged along the way.
